ma jirto gabadh ey kaga daydaan dhanka technology-da

tan ayaa ah sabab ka dhigeeso gabdhaha ine technology-da ku yaraadan, mar hadee heelenin gabdha hormuud ka ah technology-da sida raga logu haleeayo macquul ma noqoneeso gabdhaha ine bartaan ICT-ga.

Eexasho dhanka jinsiga

tan waxee sabab u noqon kartaa gabdha inesan helin shaqo ICT ah, tusaalo ahaan shirkada ugu rooon ee dalka waxe shaqoyinka kuso baahiyan aalada social media halka kuwa kale eey si private ah dadkoda ey u shaqaalesiyan, kuwa so baahiya shaqoyinka ICT ga hade gabdhah CV ey u geysato 95% waxe ku doorbidayan raga ku kalsonan la'an xirfada gabdha e ICT-ga.

Inesan heysan taagero ku filan

gabdhuhu ma heystaan taagero ku filan o ka caawiyo saro u qaadita xirfadooda ha ahaado saxiibo gabdho kale ee barte ICT-gaa, macalimaad gabdho ah amaba familyg, sido kele tan waa sabaha ugu weyn ee gabadha sabab u ah inee ku yaraadan technoloy-da.

fahandarada qoyska ee ICT-ga

qoyska aan fahansaneyd technology-da ayaa ah dhibaatoyinka ugu raadka weyn ey ee gabadhaha kala kulmaan barashada ICT-ga, tusaale gabdha ayado rabta inee barato technology-da ayee qoyska ku dhahayan rag ayaa logu talegalay ee adigu cafimadka baro o nursing noqo ICT waxba kama qaban kartidee.

sidaan ayaan lagugu tala gale

bulshada ayaa ka dhigeeso gabdha in aan logu talagalin isticmaalka technology-da, waxey u go'aamiyen kaliya inee madaxa maro ku xirato, caruurteedana ey dhaxda ku xirato kadibna ey ka shaqeeso howlaha guriga.
